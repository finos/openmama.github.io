Detailed Description

Service level of a MAMA subscription.

Provides an enumeration that represents the service level (a) requested by a subscribing application, or (b) provided by the publisher. In the future, the service level provided to the application might change dynamically, depending upon infrastructure capacity. So, even though the requested service level is "real time", if service deteriorates for any reason, the subscription may automatically switch to a conflated level (after notifying the application of the switch).
